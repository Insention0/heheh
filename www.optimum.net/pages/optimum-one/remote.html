Remote

It all starts here. Experience entertainment like never before.


Buttons





With your Bluetooth-enabled remote, you can find what you want faster by using voice commands to change channels, search for movies or shows, navigate to a menu, open an app and more.

Program your remote

Control your box

To use voice commands and control the box when it's placed out of sight, even when located inside a cabinet or entertainment center, you must first pair it.


  1. Turn on the TV you want to pair your new remote to.
  2. Press on your remote and navigate to Settings.
  3. Press to highlight "Remote" and, press SELECT, then select "Pair Remote To Altice One".
  4. Press and simultaneously on your remote for 5 seconds.
  5. Highlight "Pair Remote Control" and press SELECT.
  6. You'll see on-screen confirmation that your remote is paired.


Control your TV

In order for you to turn the TV on and off and control your TV's volume, you need to program the remote to your TV.




First, identify your TV brand code on the chart to the right: If you don't see your TV brand code on this list, click here.

  1. Next, turn on the TV you want the remote to control.
  2. Press and hold and the brand number of your TV for 3 seconds until the light at the top of the remote turns blue, then release the buttons.
  3. With the blue light on, press and hold . When the TV turns off, release . This may take a few seconds.
  4. Verify that the programming worked by pressing . This should turn the TV back on.
  5. Press SELECT to store the code. The blue light will turn off when the code is stored.

Important

Once a remote is paired to a box it can only control that particular box. If you'd like to use a paired remote on a different TV and box in your house, press and simultaneously for 5 seconds to unpair it and repeat the "Program your remote" steps above.

Voice Commands

Press and hold and speak naturally, just as you would to another person. You'll see on your TV that the microphone is on and listening for your request. When done speaking, release and your search results will quickly appear.


  • tune to a channel by network name or number:
    		•  "Watch ESPN"
  • turn on/off closed captioning:
    		•  "Turn on closed captioning"
  • open apps:
    		•  "Open YouTube"
  • navigate to menu options:
    		•  "Go to On Demand"
  • search by title for movies or TV shows:
    		•  "Friends"
  • search by actor or director:
    		•  "Tom Hanks"
  • search by genre:
    		•  "Show me kids shows"
  • search by keyword:
    		•  "Zombies"
  • find a sports teams:
    		•  "Green Bay Packers"
